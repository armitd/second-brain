# Getting Fit

**Started:** June 2026  
**Goal:** General health — more energy, feel better, sustainable habits

---

## The Vision

Not a crash programme. Build habits that stick — consistent exercise, better eating, proper sleep. The aim is to feel noticeably different by August and be genuinely fit by end of year.

## Exercise & Training

### Principles
- Start where you are, not where you think you should be
- Consistency beats intensity — three moderate sessions beats one heroic one
- Build in walking — Jurassic Coast Walk is a natural target

### Weekly Structure (to build towards)
| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| Mon | Walk or light cardio (30 min) |
| Tue | Strength (bodyweight or gym — 30–40 min) |
| Wed | Rest or stretching |
| Thu | Walk or bike — longer (45–60 min) |
| Fri | Strength (30–40 min) |
| Sat | Active — long walk, hike, swim, whatever fits the day |
| Sun | Rest |

## Diet & Nutrition

### Principles
- No extreme diets — sustainable changes only
- Focus on reducing UPF (ultra-processed food) and alcohol, not calorie obsession
- Cook more, eat out less

### Focus Areas

- [ ] Eat a proper breakfast (stops poor choices later)
- [ ] Increase protein — supports both weight loss and exercise recovery
