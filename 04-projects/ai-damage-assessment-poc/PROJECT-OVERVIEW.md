# AI Damage Assessment PoC

## What is this project?
Evaluating AI/computer vision models for automated windscreen and vehicle damage assessment at Belron. Goal is to identify the best combination of accuracy, cost, GDPR compliance, and integration feasibility for production deployment.

## Vendor Shortlist
- **DeepSeek V4-Pro** — Self-hostable, $3.48/M tokens, 1M context, #1 coding benchmark. Strong GDPR option.
- **Tractable** — Specialist automotive AI damage assessment. Market leader.
- **Ravin.ai** — AI-powered vehicle inspection platform.
- **Audatex/Solera** — Incumbent claims processing with AI features.

## Key Considerations
- GDPR compliance — preference for self-hosted or EU-data-resident options
- Cost model — closed API vs self-hosted inference costs at scale
- Integration with existing claims/FNOL workflow
- Accuracy benchmarks on windscreen-specific damage types
