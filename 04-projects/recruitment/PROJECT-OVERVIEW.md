# Recruitment

## Goal
Refresh personal brand for the job market — updated CV and LinkedIn profile that reflect current experience and seniority as an Enterprise Architect.

## Tasks

### CV
- [ ] Gather raw material — list roles, responsibilities, key achievements, and dates
- [ ] Identify target roles and level (EA, Principal Architect, CTO, etc.)
- [ ] Draft CV structure and format
- [ ] Write CV content — summary, experience, skills, education
- [ ] Review and refine with a critical eye
- [ ] Get external feedback on CV
- [ ] Finalise and export to PDF

### LinkedIn
- [ ] Audit current LinkedIn profile — note gaps and outdated content
- [ ] Update headline to reflect EA seniority and current focus
- [ ] Rewrite About section
- [ ] Update experience entries to align with CV
- [ ] Review and update skills section
- [ ] Request/refresh recommendations
- [ ] Set Open to Work or targeted recruiter visibility
