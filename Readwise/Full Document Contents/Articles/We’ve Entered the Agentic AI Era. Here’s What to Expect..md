######  👋 We're Salesforce, the customer company. CRM + Data + AI + Trust.

Everyone’s talking about AI agents, and there are so many questions:

* How are agents evolving?
* How do you manage teams of agents?
* Who will be responsible for their mistakes?
* How do you track accountability?
* How can leaders smoothly integrate them into existing workforce processes, workflows, and preferences?

Salesforce’s chief scientist  [Silvio Savarese](https://www.linkedin.com/in/silvio-savarese-97b76114?trk=article-ssr-frontend-pulse_little-mention)  [has some answers](https://www.linkedin.com/redir/redirect?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww%2Esalesforce%2Ecom%2Fblog%2Fthe-agentic-ai-era-after-the-dawn-heres-what-to-expect%2F%3Futm_campaign%3Damer_cbaw%26utm_content%3DSalesforce_LinkedIn%2520Newsletter%2520%2528Ask%2520More%2520of%2520AI%2529%26utm_medium%3Dorganic_social%26utm_source%3Dlinkedin&urlhash=7mj5&trk=article-ssr-frontend-pulse_little-text-block).
