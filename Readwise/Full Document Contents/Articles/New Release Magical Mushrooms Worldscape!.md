- Category: #articles
- Summary: The brand new "Magical Mushrooms" Focus worldscape is live today in your TRIPP app. It's one of our trippiest yet—but don't take our word for it... **experience it for yourself anytime using TRIPP Composer.** **Here's how:** - Select Composer from the main menu - In Composer, choose "Focus" - As you are selecting your worldscape, go to the "Nature" or "Psychedelic" categories to find "Magical Mushrooms" - From there, simply select an audio topic to fit your current or desired mood, and go! We are keen to keep evolving and improving TRIPP and are working on a variety of new content for you to go even deeper, including new worldscapes, games, and guided breathing practices. **We hope you enjoy "Magical Mushrooms" and let us know what you think!** We appreciate your suggestions and take them to heart.
