#### Details

The [AI Council](https://www.gov.uk/government/groups/ai-council) is an independent expert committee. It provides advice to the UK Government, as well as high-level leadership of the Artificial Intelligence (AI) ecosystem.

This independent report draws on the expertise of its members and those in its wider ecosystem to summarise four pillars on which to build the UK’s future in AI. It invites action across government to keep the UK at the forefront of safe and responsible AI.
