[Carlos Brito](https://uk.linkedin.com/in/carlos-brito1?trk=public_post_feed-actor-name) 

Last week in Orlando, [Safelite](https://www.linkedin.com/company/safelite?trk=public_post-text)'s US leaders gathered to share how innovation, talent and our ‘one team’ spirit will drive our plans and dreams for 2026 under [Renee Cacchillo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/reneecacchillo?trk=public_post-text)'s inspirational leadership.

A panel of frontline colleagues gave ideas on improvements we could make in our business; in a session on talent, leaders shared stories of their career journeys and, and we heard how we’ll use new technologies to improve our customer and colleague experiences.

I was also lucky enough to meet some of our US clients at our Customer Experience Conference where the focus was how technology – including vehicle glass technology - is rapidly shaping our future. We also heard exciting news of a new strategic AI partnership in the US with Sierra. More of this to follow shortly.

The icing on the cake, though, was seeing ten of our brilliant technicians compete in the electrifying ‘Best of Safelite’. Huge congratulations to the winner Southwest Region's Kai Rade, who will represent the US in next year’s Best of Belron – our unique and intense competition where we celebrate our frontline heroes and find the very best Belron technician in the world.
