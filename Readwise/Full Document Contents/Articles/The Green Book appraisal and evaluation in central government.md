####  Details

[The Green Book](https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/1063330/Green_Book_2022.pdf) is the government’s guidance on options appraisal and evaluation.

From 1 November 2023, Ministers of the Crown have a legal duty to have due regard to the environmental principles policy statement when making policy. Where the duty applies, it is relevant for appraisals and evaluations that are conducted in line with the Green Book. This duty will be reflected in the Green Book when the document is next updated. Further information can also be found in the guidance on [Enabling a Natural Capital Approach](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/enabling-a-natural-capital-approach-enca). The Green Book also contains details of the other statutory duties with which appraisals must comply.
