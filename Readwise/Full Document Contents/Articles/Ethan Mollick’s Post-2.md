ChatGPT 5.2: "Build an interactive Excel spreadsheet where I can pick two D&D monsters to fight against each other and the spreadsheet simulates the combat somehow, including special abilities. Give a D&D look" Thinking took 60 minutes(!) but it created everything itself, including some very involved formulas. I had to have it fix an error that came up when I opened it, which it did well. The result, again all planned and executed by the AII, is a pretty impressive, multi-sheet setup with complex formulas and interesting solutions to hard problems.
