####  Putting people first is key to accelerating progress towards a sustainable world.

Improving the health, safety and well-being of your employees can positively impact the success and competitiveness of your organization, as well as benefitting the wider community.

Flourishing workers are at the centre of resilient organizations. Building sustainable organizational resilience means not only protecting physical and psychological health, but actively enhancing it by creating a culture of trust that delivers the essential need for fulfilment.
