## Full Document
Cheap and tasty, sausages are a great choice for an easy supper. We've brought together all the favourites and a few more adventurous options, if you fancy something a little different.

[Chipchip 'cassoulet'](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/chipchip_cassoulet_67875)

by Mary Berry

Chipolata sausages are often overlooked in favour of big juicy sausages, but they are perfect for this smoky, spicy take on a cassoulet as they don’t release too much fat and cook quickly. This is a great dish for feeding hungry people after a long winter walk.
