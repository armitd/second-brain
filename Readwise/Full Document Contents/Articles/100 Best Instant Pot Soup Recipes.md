## Full Document
From healthy chicken soups to hearty beef stews, there are plenty of **instant pot soup recipes** to choose from.

![100 Best Instant Pot Soup Recipes](https://www.prudentpennypincher.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/instant-pot-soup-recipes-2020.jpg)
Warm yourself up these comforting **Instant Pot soup recipes** that are perfect for cold days during soup season. Whether you are looking for creamy soups or hearty soups, there are a lot of great recipes for your electric pressure cooker. There are chicken, beef, pork, turkey and vegetarian soups. These homemade soups make easy and quick weeknight meals and [meal prep ideas](https://www.prudentpennypincher.com/50-best-meal-prep-recipes/) for the week. My favorite thing about these soup recipes is that there isn’t a lot of time involved compared to cooking on the stove top so it’s less time spent in the kitchen.
