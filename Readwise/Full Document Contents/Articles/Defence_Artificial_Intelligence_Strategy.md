- Summary: The Defence Artificial Intelligence Strategy sets out how the UK will adopt and exploit AI to transform Defence into an 'AI ready' organisation and deliver cutting-edge capability. It aims to build stronger partnerships with the UK's AI industry and collaborate internationally to promote security, stability and democratic values. Objectives include upskilling leaders and workforce, adopting and exploiting AI at pace and scale, and strengthening the UK's defence and security AI ecosystem. The Strategy defines AI as a family of general-purpose technologies that may enable machines to perform tasks traditionally requiring human or biological intelligence.
