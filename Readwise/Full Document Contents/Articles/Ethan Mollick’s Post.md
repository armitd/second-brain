To get an idea of the near-term future of work with AI, take a look at the official Claude Cowork plugins, which give the AI specialized knowledge for various hard tasks

A natural successor for GPTs, but built for agents (& therefore much more scalable & customizable for firms). If you know a field well, you can probably edit or write your own skill which, since it can be run many times by many people, can have a large impact. And they are written in plain language in the way that an experienced non-technical expert would understand. Organizational skill directories are a potentially very valuable way to deploy agents inside companies.
