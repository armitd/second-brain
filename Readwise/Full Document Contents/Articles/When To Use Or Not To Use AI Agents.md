- Summary: AI agents are autonomous software that perceive, decide, act, and pursue goals. Agentic AI means solutions built from one or more such agents and ranges from emerging to advanced capability levels. Use agents when a task needs adaptable, automated decisioning; avoid them when requirements are too simple, too hard, or vendors aren’t ready.
