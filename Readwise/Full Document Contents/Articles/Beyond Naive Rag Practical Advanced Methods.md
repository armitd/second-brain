- Summary: This mini-book collects advanced talks showing that naive single-vector RAG is outdated but retrieval is still crucial. Authors describe better methods: instruction-trained retrievers, reasoning rerankers, late-interaction models, and multi-representation routing. They call for stronger evaluation and tools for hard tasks like long contexts, out-of-domain data, and reasoning-heavy queries.
