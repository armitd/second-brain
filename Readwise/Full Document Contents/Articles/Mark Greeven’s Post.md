Professor of Management Innovation & Influential Voice on China Innovation & Dean of IMD Asia

🔍 Trying to make sense of China’s AI after hearing about DeepSeek?

Here is a snapshot of China's AI ecosystem! This map by [IMD](https://ch.linkedin.com/school/imd-business-school/?trk=public_post-text) reveals how China is shaping the future of artificial intelligence across multiple sectors.

What caught my eye:  

- The diversity of players beyond the tech giants  

- Strong focus on practical applications: from healthcare to autonomous driving  

- Deep integration of AI in education and finance

What is next? Spatial AI and robotics

Most intriguing is how traditional sectors are being transformed through AI innovation. The ecosystem shows a healthy ecosystem of established tech giants, ambitious startups, and academic powerhouses.

What stands out to you in this AI landscape? Are there sectors you think will see the most dramatic transformation in the next few years?
