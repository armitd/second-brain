##### GETTING HERE

 Barnes Station approx 10 min walk or Bus 33 to Red Lion / Church Road

Barnes Bridge Station approx 10 min walk or Bus 533/209/378 to Red Lion / Church Road

 
 533 - Hammersmith Underground to Red Lion / Church Road 

419 - Richmond to Red Lion / Church Road

378 - Putney to Red Lion / Church Road

209 - Mortlake to Hammersmith Bridge via Red Lion / Church Road

 
 There is free parking after midday in various residential streets, please check Richmond Council website for more information. We are based on Church Road which has Pay & Display on-street parking Mon-Sat 0800-1800. 

See our parking zones [here](https://www.richmond.gov.uk/services/parking/cpz/cpz_times)
