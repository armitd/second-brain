Did you know our [BattleLab](https://uk.linkedin.com/company/battlelab?trk=public_post-text) ecosystem has been uploaded onto our Co Collaboration space on Defence Ideas 💡

Some of the Features of the Virtual Environment:

💢 Provide a space for you to let Military, Academic and Industry know who you are and what you can offer.

💢 We have linked all available and known organisations that can support you on your Innovation journey (if you know of more, let us know, and we will add).

💢 Book the physical space and sign up for coffee mornings.

💢 Engage with a variety of National and International challenges; we have a number currently available and being hosted by the platform from Australian and UK MOD.

💢 Newsroom for better understanding of activities you can get involved with.

💢 Chat areas to inform others when you are going to be available at the physical space to allow for better collaboration.

Join us on the platform; we have tried to keep it simple and have the same low barrier to entry as the physical space.

A High-Resolution image has been uploaded to the Defence BattleLab 'ask the team area' (it's from March, a new update will see some amendments to reflect recent connections).

Join us! Link in the comments! 👇
