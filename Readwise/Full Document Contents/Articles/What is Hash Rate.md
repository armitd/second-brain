####  Discover what hash rate and hash power is, why it's important to know and how it can help you to mine cryptocurrency.

 
[Hash rate](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/hash-rate/) (sometimes called the hash power) is the speed at which a [cryptocurrency](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/cryptocurrency/) [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) device operates.

To be successful at [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/), speed is of the essence since the [miner](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/miner/) is trying to solve a question, add a [block](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/block/) the the chain and reap the rewards before anyone else. The more answers suggested over the shortest period of time will increase the chances of solving that [block](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/block/).

#### **What is the cryptographic hash function?**

A hash function in computer processing is a function that compresses information, meaning the output is generally shorter than the input. Hash functions are used in [many parts of cryptography](https://coinrivet.com/what-is-cryptography-and-why-is-it-important/) to map data of differing sizes to data of a fixed size.

Cryptographic hash functions are ideal for processing [cryptocurrency](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/cryptocurrency/) transactions. They are secure and are widely used in information security applications involving authentication, digital signatures and message authentication codes. In the context of [cryptocurrency](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/cryptocurrency/) [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/), a hash is one computation or “guess” at solving a [block](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/block/). [The hash rate can increase](https://bitcoinist.com/bitcoin-hash-rate-4-month-optimism/) or decrease.

####  **Laptops vs miners**

These days it’s impossible to mine [Bitcoin](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/bitcoin/) using a standard desktop computer. The computational difficulty of [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) is too high. When [Bitcoin](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/bitcoin/) first arrived it was possible to mine using a standard home computer or, more specifically, [the computer’s GPU (graphics processing unit](https://coinrivet.com/guides/what-is-cryptocurrency-mining/the-best-gpus-for-cryptocurrency-mining/)).

A GPU is a part of the video rendering system. The work it does is very repetitive and deals with a high volume of information. This kind of bulky mathematical work can be done by a GPU much better and in a much higher volume than the computer’s CPU (central processing unit).

#### **What are the different hash rates?**

That number is so big we need to talk megas, gigas and teras. These are the hash rates you will see being talked about in the context of [cryptocurrency](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/cryptocurrency/) [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/).

* 1 kH/s\* (one kilo hash) is 1,000 (one thousand) hashes per second
* 1 MH/s (one mega hash) is 1,000,000 (one million) hashes per second
* 1 GH/s (one giga hash) is 1,000,000,000 (one billion) hashes per second
* 1 TH/s (one tera hash) is 1,000,000,000,000 (one trillion) hashes per second
* 1 PH/s (one peta hash) is 1,000,000,000,000,000 (one quadrillion) hashes per second
* 1 EH/s (one exa hash) is 1,000,000,000,000,000,000 (one quintillion) hashes per second
* 1 ZH/s (one zeta hash) is 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 (one sextillion) hashes per second
* 1 YH/s (one yotta hash) is 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 (one septillion) hashes per second

**\* kH/s is always written with a lower case k as upper case K is reserved for Kelvin (which is a measurement of heat).**

#### **Hash rate arms race**

It isn’t hard to see that [cryptocurrency](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/cryptocurrency/) [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) is becoming progressively more difficult. In the case of [Bitcoin](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/bitcoin/), this is deliberate. The difficulty of [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) is constantly adjusted to keep the production to a fixed time period.

The more miners attracted to the [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) effort then the more difficult it becomes. To be successful at [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) requires more hash power to have any chance of sharing the rewards.

To see how much effort and power is required in [mining](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/mining/) [Bitcoin](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/bitcoin/), for example, you should use a [hash rate](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/hash-rate/) calculator. To use this, you’ll need to know:

* Hashing power
* Power consumption
* Cost per KWh
* Pool Fee

In return for inputting this data, you’ll discover how much profit you can make per day, how much [Bitcoin](https://coinrivet.com/dictionary/bitcoin/) you can mine, and how much your power consumption will cost you.
