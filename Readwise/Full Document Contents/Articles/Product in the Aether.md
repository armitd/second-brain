## Full Document
A remote meet-up about product management and product thinking, bringing together people from any location to discuss all things product. (Did we mention product?) This could be people, process, prioritisation (or prioritization), design, research, stakeholder management, ethics, agile, lean, teams, delivery, etc. The group decides, on the day, what we’re going to talk about.

##### Where is it?

The meet-up is held online over video (we use [zoom](https://zoom.us/)), which means it is wherever you are. You don’t need a zoom account, but you will need to install the software.

##### What do I need to take part?

* a computer with access you can install zoom on
* decent internet connection to cope with a video call
* a webcam and microphone so everyone can see and hear you
* quiet space and headphones so you can hear everyone
* a desire to learn and share

Take a look at the [remote meeting tips](http://agileintheether.co.uk/remote-meeting-tips/) to learn more

##### Do I need to be a product manager to take part?

No. That would be boring.

But you *do* need to be interested in the types of things product managers are likely to care about - what [Seb Sabouné calls Product Thinking](https://vimeo.com/383992991/1aaaab8489).

Some events may be targeted at specific topics or for people in similar situations.

This event uses the *in the Ether* format. For more info or to find out how to run your own meet-up *in the Ether* please see [intheether.xyz](http://intheether.xyz)
