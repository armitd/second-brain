#### A study revealed what today’s AI-powered organizations are doing differently to achieve success.

I’ve just gone through the fourth edition of the Deloitte AI Institute’s “The State of AI in the Enterprise” survey.

It is impressive how it investigates the more profound transformations occurring within organizations using AI to drive value to understand what the most “AI-fueled organizations” are doing to drive success.

The report surveyed 2,875 executives from 11 Americas, Europe, and Asia between March and May 2021.

It explored how AI-powered organizations are using data as a resource to systematically deploy and scale AI across all types of core business processes in a human-centered manner.

This report helps us have a more accurate view of the leading practices aligned with the marketplace leaders across industries.

The report’s key findings help us understand how to overcome challenges to becoming an AI-fueled organization, regardless of the stage of AI transformation we are in, and primarily to assist those earlier in their transformation.
