Is Blockchain now firmly entering the "Trough of disillusionment" ? Three major projects have announced that they're either insolvent or discontinuing:

1 Latest news is that the long running Australia Securities Exchange blockchain project to replace the existing CHESS platform is being terminated.

[https://lnkd.in/eFjg7HvV](https://lnkd.in/eFjg7HvV?trk=public_post-text)

2 The Reinsurance industry B3i consortia became insolvent earlier this year:

[https://lnkd.in/ebZBU97K](https://lnkd.in/ebZBU97K?trk=public_post-text)

3 [we.trade](http://we.trade?trk=public_post-text), the joint-venture company owned by 12 European banks and IBM, with shareholders including CaixaBank, Deutsche Bank, Erste Group, HSBC, KBC, Nordea, Rabobank, Santander, Société Générale, UBS and UniCredit. Initially built by IBM, the [we.trade](http://we.trade?trk=public_post-text) platform is powered by Hyperledger Fabric, and is currently licensed by 16 banks across 15 countries. In June it was reported that it was unable to secure further funding and was winding down.
