Contact centers are under pressure to reduce operating costs while meeting customers’ ever-increasing expectations, but their efforts to address this with AI are hampered by [disconnected systems](https://www.salesforce.com/content/dam/web/en_us/www/documents/e-books/service/state-of-service-7th-edition.pdf). Customer data lives in silos, separated from the context across the channels and systems that humans and AI agents need to act. Legacy [contact center](https://www.salesforce.com/service/contact-center/guide/) tools rely on slow, costly custom integrations to stitch together CRM and AI platforms. Without a unified foundation that connects data, systems, and AI natively, contact centers can’t deliver on reducing costs or improving service with AI.

Today, Salesforce introduced [Agentforce Contact Center](https://www.salesforce.com/service/contact-center/), the only contact center solution that unifies voice, digital channels, CRM data, and [AI agents](https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/ai-agents/) natively in a single system, enabling customer self-service at scale, seamless AI-to-human handoffs, and real-time visibility across every interaction. Every agent pulls from the same source of truth, so they can proactively understand the customer’s complete history and incorporate insights from voice conversations, chats, texts, past purchases or marketing activity into every interaction. And because Agentforce Contact Center is built on [Salesforce’s unified platform](https://www.salesforce.com/platform/), there’s no need for expensive, time-consuming integrations, resulting in intelligent, integrated service able to meet customer demands without the added operating costs.

“Managing support across professional installers, retail partners, and direct consumers requires a contact center solution that understands context,” said **Beth LeClerc, VP of Business Systems Architecture & Web Services at Savant Systems.** “Agentforce Contact Center’s ability to intelligently route customers and provide our agents with the right information for each interaction — whether it’s a luxury home installer or a DIY smart home enthusiast — ensures we deliver premium service across every touchpoint of our business.”

#### **A New Standard for Service**

In this unified solution, AI agents resolve more cases autonomously, and help to ensure that only the highest-priority or most complex issues are escalated to humans. When these handoffs occur, human agents receive the full context of the interaction, allowing them to pick up exactly where AI agents left off. By eliminating context gaps, tool switching, and data siloes, teams can achieve outcomes like increased first-touch resolution, decreased average handle time, and increased customer satisfaction.

Now, human and AI agents don’t just answer questions; they take action, and every customer interaction becomes smarter and more personalized.

* **Enable Smarter Agents Across Every Channel:** [Agentforce](https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/?d=cta-body-promo-8) knows your customer’s needs before they do, because it understands every interaction from sales and marketing to service. These agents work in sync to complete tasks on behalf of customers, like rebooking a flight or updating a billing cycle. Customers no longer have to repeat a single detail. When humans and AI agents both work in the same CRM, they have the full picture of the customer’s journey.
* **Create Seamless Handoffs:** When a case is too complex for AI, the handoff to a human is finally more seamless. Because every channel is native and connected to your sales, service, and marketing data, your human agents receive the full transcript and customer history instantly. They pick up exactly where the AI left off, helping to ensure that the customer never has to repeat a single detail.

* **Unlock the Value of Voice Data:** By making voice native to the CRM, Agentforce Contact Center automatically bridges the gap between spoken conversation and customer records, capturing nuances in real time. * **Launch One Workspace for Everyone:** The whole contact center team works in a single view, and supervisors manage the entire operation from one dashboard. You can build an agent once and deploy it across every channel, including voice, to accelerate your AI rollout while keeping routing rules consistent for both your AI and human teams.
