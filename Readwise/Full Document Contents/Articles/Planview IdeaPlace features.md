- Summary: Planview IdeaPlace helps organizations streamline innovation by unifying idea management through various templates and tools. ## Full Document
* Unify your organization’s innovation activity into a single value delivery ecosystem.

	+ ##### Challenge Templates
	
	 Broadcast challenges & events, capture ideas, discover opportunities, and solve problems using preconfigured or custom templates to explore specific innovation use cases easily and quickly.
	+ ##### Guides & Tutorials
	
	 Solicit advice and discover best practices through in-app chat with customer care, help center content, embedded videos & articles, and guided feature walkthroughs.
	+ ##### Mobile-Enabled Innovation
	
	 Capture inspiration in real time: Access challenges, post ideas, vote, comment, collaborate, or perform other user activities during your commute or on the go.
	+ ##### External Innovation
	
	 Extend engagement to key external crowds with easy access to the system through social media credentials.
	+ ##### Idea to Portfolio
	
	 Create projects and products in Planview Portfolios that flow your best ideas directly to portfolio prioritization, funding, and delivery.
	+ ##### Idea to Project
	
	 Create projects in Planview AdaptiveWork that flow your best ideas directly to resourcing, collaboration and execution capabilities.
	+ ##### Idea to Delivery Teams
	
	 Connect to Planview ProjectPlace through the idea to workspace connector, helping to connect ideas to delivery and incubation teams.
* Build an organic culture of innovation, where high-impact ideas can come from anyone.

	+ ##### TeamTap
	
	 Democratize the ideation process by enabling users to create and manage challenges their own way—without the need for an admin, templates, or an admin panel.
	+ ##### Social Activity Trends
	
	 Leverage popular features of social media to build community and increase engagement in ways that help cultivate ideas, rather than just collect them.
	+ ##### Crowd Predictions
	
	 Incorporate diversity of thought and opinion to help prioritize ideas by allowing users to estimate revenue, cost and delivery time required for an idea.
* Data-driven innovation management from idea to impact.

	+ ##### Guided User Experience
	
	 Personalize guidance ensuring that each user is only presented with fresh content—all made available in a variety of languages.
	+ ##### User Skill Interests
	
	 Enable users to add skills and interests to their profiles and use that data to build effective teams and to push challenges to specific skillsets.
	+ ##### Idea Capture Forms
	
	 Capture ideas with build-your-own-idea forms that start with a standard format and include a wide variety of configurable options for customization.
	+ ##### Idea Evaluation & Reviews
	
	 Leverage automation to push prioritized ideas to appropriate subject matter experts who compare and evaluate them to narrow the field and drive workflow.
	+ ##### Idea Workflow Automation
	
	 Leverage automation to manage idea workflows from crowd selection to subject matter experts, and use scoring methods to prioritize them, removing the need to review every idea.
* Measure the impact of emerging trends and sentiment across innovation engagements.

	+ ##### Smart Idea Consolidation
	
	 Use natural language processing to identify and consolidate comparable ideas to keep the crowd focused on the strongest concepts—all without admin interaction.
	+ ##### Trends & Sentiment
	
	 Quickly explore millions of lines of text and sentiment using advanced natural language processing and analysis capabilities. Identify key trends and themes to seize opportunities and deliver value to your customers using a data-driven innovation process.
	+ ##### Executive Reporting
	
	 Maximize business intelligence with embedded Microsoft Power BI, standard & customized dashboards, reporting and built-in ML powered analytics to illustrate and evaluate all elements of your programs.
* Combine data, technology, and wisdom from the crowd as the foundation for data-driven evaluation.

	+ ##### Flexible Configuration
	
	 Get started fast with preconfigured templates for consistency, or customize each challenge through an admin panel equipped with embedded guides for assistance.
	+ ##### Idea Voting & Commenting
	
	 Gather crowd feedback through public or private comments by collecting social media style “likes,” or by allowing users to rate ideas on a star-based system.
	+ ##### Engagement Gamification
	
	 Leverage embedded gamification elements like badges and points to drive engagement, and a reputation tool to evaluate each user’s participation.
	+ ##### Pairwise Idea Ranking
	
	 Rank ideas at any time based solely on merit and without bias through a sophisticated branching algorithm that uses prior user assessments to determine pairings.
