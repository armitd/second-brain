Leadership in a VUCA world – case Formula 1! If you’re like me you don’t associate motor sports with business agility. So when I was approached to illustrate and summarise a book about the topic ([The Future Business Formula)](https://www.sullivanstanley.com/futurebusinessformula/) I was a wee bit skeptical. But holy moly was I in for a surprise!

Formula 1 has indeed managed to reinvent itself. Also, I was impressed by the story of the racing team Red Bull that showed what you can achieve when you give competent people autonomy rather than try to impose unnecessary hierarchy on them. Customer centricity, iterative work, feedback loops and self-management all feature.
