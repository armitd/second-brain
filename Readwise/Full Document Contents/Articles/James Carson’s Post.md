This [Liquid Death](https://www.linkedin.com/company/liquid-death?trk=public_post-text) advert was made entirely with AI in Veo 3.

[Amir Ariely 🇮🇱](https://www.linkedin.com/in/arielyoriginal?trk=public_post-text) created it... it's really very good. I couldn't tell that it wasn't real initially.

Also, it's funny - it's got a great script. Although I must admit I was slightly stumped about the ending.

Most of the other AI generated ads I've seen are high octane mashups, or just people standing around in branded stuff. Ie, you can tell they're AI.

But this has taken it up another gear.

I can only see this going in one direction > advertising industry disruption.

I think if you're in commercial video production, it's time you looked into this!
