#### **Seven Rules of The Metaverse**

In many ways, the metaverse is typically another name for metamorphosing the internet – to be more immersive, social, secure, and far more economically sophisticated as compared to what exists today. On a broader scale, there are two competing visions of how to realize this change:

* The first vision is decentralized, open, interoperable, generous with new frontiers and property rights, and owned by the communities that essentially build and maintain it.
* The second vision is centralized which is a lot more familiar to many people today. It is closed, subjected to the whims of third parties and corporations, and often extracts economic rent from its inhabitants, creators, and contributors.

While the concept is entirely dependent on the business model, scale, and nature of services, both centralized and decentralized metaverses exist in the present landscape. However, considering the profound benefits, opportunities, and scope that the decentralized vision presents are relatively unmatched and unrivaled. There are several principles and attributes that govern the ‘true’ metaverse that is decentralized and accessible to all.

In this blog, we will shed some light on the [seven core rules that define the metaverse](https://medium.com/meta-verses/the-seven-rules-of-the-metaverse-7d4e06fa864c). We will also elucidate the implications of how these principles relate to businesses and the general public alike. So let us begin.

**Understanding The Seven Rules of The Metaverse** 

While we actually can now foresee what shape the metaverse will possibly take in the coming years, however, we are sure that it will be absolutely revolutionizing. We believe that someday an expansive global network of spatially organized, predominant, and hyper-realistic 3D content will be available to everyone without any restriction. It will be used in every possible human endeavor as a new and profoundly transformative medium, enabled by mega innovations in human-computer interface, hardware, creator tools, network infrastructure, and digital economies.

Here are the seven rules that encapsulate the borderless metaverse. Please note that a metaverse does not exclusively need to frame these principles, however, these rules signify what a metaverse entirely means and how it functions in general.

1. There is only one metaverse.
2. The metaverse is open.
3. The metaverse is for everyone.
4. No one controls the metaverse.
5. The metaverse is a network.
6. The metaverse is hardware-independent.
7. The metaverse is the Internet.

**Explanation of The Seven Rules of The Metaverse**

**Rule 1**

**There is only one metaverse**

It is an undeniable fact that there is only one metaverse. It is the sum total of all publicly accessible virtual realms, 3D real-time content, and related digital media that are closely connected on an open global network that is accessible to all and controlled by none.

* The term ‘a metaverse’ is typically not appropriate when referring to any place within the metaverse. In case of referring to any place in the metaverse, the correct terms are ‘virtual world’, ‘world’, ‘node’, ‘space’, ‘environment’, ‘zone’, or other similar terminologies. In a similar way, the term ‘metaverses’ is not appropriate when referring to multiple such places within the metaverse.

* In case anyone refers to such a place being a ‘metaverse’, then they are not describing the metaverse. They are essentially denoting a theme park, a game, or a walled garden community that inculcates many attributes similar to the metaverse.

**Rule 2**

**The metaverse is open**

The metaverse is essentially open. It is architected upon interoperable technologies as well as tools, connected through broadly agreed-upon and rigorously defined open and free communications standards.

* Open, secure, and interoperable technologies offer the most assured way to scale the metaverse. On the other hand, proprietary technologies and closed systems have inherent limitations to scale, in spite of conferring short-term merits.

* An open ecosystem of hardware and software provides greater flexibility and choices to users and content creators. Greater choice means more and more engaging and immersive content, more opportunities for the tool as well as platform providers, and more success for creators, enabling a virtuous cycle.

**Rule 3**

**The metaverse is for everyone**

As defined by our broad societal rules of inclusion, the metaverse is for everyone. This does not imply any socioeconomic or political statement, however, it does imply generic accessibility and openness of the metaverse to the general public.

* While being a predominant type of experience in the metaverse, virtual environments are not essentially a defining characteristic or a precondition. All media types including 3D objects are incorporated; the information architecture and presentation style is in service to the use case.

* The metaverse is not merely a video game or a network of video games. While much of the interaction mechanics, production value, and economics within the metaverse will be ‘game-like’, however, there are a plethora of other experiences that define the metaverse.

* Since the metaverse inculcates the broadest set of use cases, it vastly accommodates and caters to the requirements of its creators and users across societies, businesses, and academia.

**Rule 4**

**No one controls the metaverse**

Nobody controls the infinite metaverse. It is essentially the universal commons for digital commerce and communication, governed as needed for the common interest, intermediated as requirements dictate, and functions towards the greatest good for all.

* Any metaverse that is controlled by a single party will be inherently limited in terms of scaling because of the free market competition. Usually, users seek the best value and widest range of options.

* The forces of decentralization are in focal play as well as in gathering power. NFTs, DeFi, DAOs, and other blockchain-based infrastructures have moved beyond experimentation to an early deployment phase. Moreover, Web3 technology has already become an early foundation of [metaverse development services](https://www.oodlestechnologies.com/metaverse-development/) and will continue to play a critical role in the metaverse.

**Rule 5**

**The metaverse is a network**

The metaverse is entirely a computer network connecting the publicly accessible environments across the globe along with real-time 3D content as well as related media.

* The metaverse is not a computer program, rather, it is a set of multiple computer programs that processes information exchange through network protocols.

* The metaverse is not an access point or a specific onramp. There will be search engines, directories, portals, and other such solutions that assist users in locating places and traversing the expansive metaverse – however, all these are just the maps of the metaverse and not the metaverse itself.

**Rule 6**

**The metaverse is hardware-independent**

The metaverse is independent of hardware or gadgets. It is accessible on any device irrespective of its form factor or display type.

* Accessibility within the metaverse is paramount. Content should be built and designed keeping accessibility in mind. In addition to not depending on particular user interface techniques and device types, experiences should be formulated flexibly in order to accommodate users with impairments and disabilities. Remember, the metaverse is for everyone.

* A defining attribute of the metaverse is spatially organized, with predominant real-time 3D content. For the content and virtual experiences to be accessible to all, they must be able to be presented on a multitude of hardware, metaverse gadgets, wearable devices, and devices that are already in users’ possession.

**Rule 7**

**The metaverse is the internet**

Basically, the metaverse is the internet, upgraded and enhanced to continually deliver 3D content, real-time synchronous communication, and spatially organized information & immersive experiences.

* Conversely, the internet is NOT the metaverse, not typically yet. The internet of today is progressing its way towards greater possibilities, opportunities, and potential, and will form the basis of the metaverse of tomorrow.

* The current Web3 innovations and the movement toward decentralization, for instance, are pivotal steps toward putting the power back in the hands of creators and consumers for secure payment, content distribution, and identity verification.
