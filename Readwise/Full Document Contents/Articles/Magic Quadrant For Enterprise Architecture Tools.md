- Summary: Gartner evaluated vendors of enterprise architecture (EA) tools, defining EA tools as platforms that model and analyze an organization’s applications, capabilities, processes, and technology to support decision-making and roadmaps. The market is shifting with mergers, new entrants, and larger vendors growing fastest, while vendors vary in product maturity, features, and integration. Gartner used five key EA use cases and strict inclusion criteria to place vendors as Leaders, Visionaries, Niche Players, or Honorable Mentions.
