The toolkit operates through expertly designed prompt chains utilizing the ChatGPT API. Once you input your initial information, the toolkit initiates an expert prompt chain that processes your data and generates an insights report and ultimately you decide on the generated insights.

Details on JTBD  

JTBD (job to be done) is a framework for viewing your products and solutions in terms of the "jobs" customers are trying to get done. In other words, the JTBD is the reason your customers "hire" your product or service.
