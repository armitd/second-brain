Today, we’re open-sourcing the draft specification for [DESIGN.md](http://DESIGN.md), so it can be used across any tool or platform. We’re also adding new capabilities.

[DESIGN.md](http://DESIGN.md) lets you easily export and import your design rules from project to project. Instead of guessing intent, agents know exactly what a color is for and can even validate their choices against WCAG accessibility rules.
