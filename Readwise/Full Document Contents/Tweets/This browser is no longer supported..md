🔷 Tibialis Anterior Raises

• Lean back against a wall  
• Pull toes up and toward your body  
• Build to single leg for an added challenge  
• Move feet farther away from the wall to increase difficulty

• Rise high up onto toes  
• Single leg for added difficulty  
• Return down slowly to strengthen Achilles

🔷 Bulgarian Split Squat

• Use a knee-high platform  
• Keep standing foot flat on the ground  
• Lean your upper body slightly forward  
• Lower rear knee down toward the ground  
• Front knee bends to about 90 degrees

🔷 Hip Hikes

• Stand on one leg  
• Hold onto something to balance  
• Movement is at your standing hip  
• Your floating leg moves up and down  
• Should feel effort at the front of your hip

🔷 Bridge

• Lie on your back, knees bent  
• Keep your feet flat on the floor  
• Raise your hips up off the ground  
• At the top keep your body straight
