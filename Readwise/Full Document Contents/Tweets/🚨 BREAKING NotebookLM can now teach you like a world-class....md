## Full Document
🚨 BREAKING: NotebookLM can now teach you like a world-class private tutor — without the $150/hr price tag. For free. 🤯

These 8 prompts can replace hours of studying, coaching, and expensive tutoring sessions.

Save this before everyone starts using it. 

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIm29D5a0AAzNFV.jpg?name=orig)U5 

---

1️⃣ Instant Study Guide 📚

Upload your notes, PDFs, or lecture slides, then prompt:

“Create a complete study guide from my material.

Organize everything by topic and include:

• 3 key concepts

• 2 exam-style questions with answers”

👉 Instant exam-ready notes. 

---

2️⃣ Socratic Quiz 🧠

Prompt:

“Quiz me on the most important concepts.

Start easy → then increase difficulty.

After each answer, explain what I got right or wrong.”

👉 Feels like a real private tutor. 

---

3️⃣ Exam Forecaster 🎯

Prompt:

“Analyze my material and predict:

• Top 5 likely exam topics

• Why they matter

• 1 hard question for each”

👉 Study smarter, not longer. 

---

4️⃣ Confusion Fixer 💡

Prompt:

“Explain [concept] using:

• A simple analogy

• A real-world example

• A common mistake

Then quiz me.”

👉 Perfect for “I still don’t get it” moments. 

---

5️⃣ Gap Finder 🔍

Prompt:

“Analyze my material and identify:

• Missing concepts

• Weak areas

• Quick explanations to improve them”

👉 Finds the gaps most students never notice. 

---

6️⃣ Practice Exam Generator 📝

Prompt:

“Create a full practice exam with:

• MCQs

• Short-answer questions

• Essay questions

Include answers and explanations.”

👉 Practice like it’s the real exam. 

---

7️⃣ Audio Study Partner 🎧

Use NotebookLM’s Audio Overview feature.

👉 Turns your notes into a podcast

👉 Learn while walking, commuting, or relaxing 

---

8️⃣ Teach-Back Test 🧠

Prompt:

“I’ll explain a concept.

Tell me:

• What I got right

• What I missed

• How to improve”

👉 One of the fastest ways to truly learn.
