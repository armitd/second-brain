# Braindump: Reflexive AI Usage Is Now a Baseline — Tobi Lutke (Shopify CEO Memo)

## Raw Thoughts

This is the actual Shopify CEO memo, not commentary. Tobi Lutke shared it publicly after it began leaking. Six mandates, the most significant of which are:

1. **Reflexive AI usage is now a baseline expectation at Shopify.** Not optional. Not encouraged. Baseline.
2. **AI must be part of the prototype phase of any project.** Every GSD project's prototype phase should be dominated by AI exploration.
3. **AI usage questions added to performance and peer review questionnaires.** It is now measured.
4. **Before asking for more headcount and resources, teams must demonstrate why they cannot get what they want done using AI.** This is the hardest one. It inverts the default assumption.
5. **Everyone means everyone.** Including Tobi and the executive team.

The framing throughout is that using AI well is a skill — learned by using it a lot, unlike everything else. The memo distinguishes between people who "give up after writing a prompt and not getting the ideal thing back immediately" and people who learn to prompt and load context properly.

The 10x framing: people who use AI reflexively aren't 10% more productive. Some are 100x. That's the variance Tobi is trying to close across the company.

The red queen framing: Shopify grows 20-40% year over year. To re-qualify at the same level each year, you must improve by at least that. AI is what makes that possible without proportional headcount growth.

## Content Analysis

### Main Themes
1. **AI as baseline, not differentiator:** Once Shopify mandates this, companies that don't are at a structural disadvantage. This becomes an industry norm within 12-24 months.
2. **Headcount reframe:** "Hire an AI before a human" isn't cost-cutting language — it's productivity language. The question isn't "do we have enough people?" but "have we fully leveraged the agents available?"
3. **Measurement matters:** Adding AI usage to performance reviews makes it a concrete expectation, not a cultural aspiration. Measurement is how you operationalise a mandate.
