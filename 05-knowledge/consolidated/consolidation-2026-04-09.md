## Cross-Cutting Insights

**The week's thinking forms one coherent thread:**
Passive at work → need stakeholder visibility → visibility requires artefacts → artefacts require business understanding → business understanding requires stakeholder conversations → conversations build the visibility. The loop is self-reinforcing, and every piece of work this week feeds into it.

**Strategic AI positioning for EA:**
The agentic AI wave (MCP, A2A, Copilot agents, agentic architectures) is moving faster than most EA functions are tracking. The opportunity — and the risk — is that business units will start building agent capabilities before governance exists. The EA function should claim this territory proactively: own the MCP/A2A standards conversation, own the agent identity governance question, own the Copilot agent design.
