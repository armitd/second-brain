# Thread: ChatGPT is the iPhone. Claude is the Mac — 8 Power Prompts

## Summary
A practical thread of 8 structured prompts for turning Claude into a persistent, personalised AI system rather than a one-shot question-answerer. The core argument: ChatGPT is optimised for the casual user; Claude responds to a calibrated operating profile that makes it behave like an extension of you across a full conversation. Covers writing, decision-making, document synthesis, and weekly AI audits.

## Key Points
- **Persistent Brain Prompt:** Give Claude a full operating profile — role, communication style, writing voice, pet peeves, and working rules — so it maintains context and persona for the whole session
- **Writing Partner:** Deconstruct great writing first, then replicate the technique in your voice (not the original author's)
- **Multi-Role Team:** Prompt Claude to play 5 sequential roles — Strategist, Researcher, Writer, Editor, Distributor — and not advance until you confirm each stage
- **Senior Critique:** Ask for the surgical takedown, not praise — specific weaknesses, "tells" of junior work, what a senior would have done differently
- **Decision Framework:** Type 1 vs Type 2 decision, cognitive bias check, 10/10/10 rule, mentor's exact words — structured decision-making rather than suggestion lists
- **Document Synthesis:** Executive synthesis vs summary — extract the implicit argument, the strongest counterargument, and the action it implies
- **Knowledge Pipeline:** Process any content through a 7-step pipeline: key ideas, spaced repetition question, follow-up topic, tweetable version
- **Weekly AI Audit:** Review Claude sessions weekly — what gave ROI, what was busywork, what to systematise next

## Why It Matters
These prompts are immediately applicable to COG usage and general AI literacy. The "persistent brain" prompt is essentially a manual version of what COG does automatically via MY-PROFILE.md and CLAUDE.md.
