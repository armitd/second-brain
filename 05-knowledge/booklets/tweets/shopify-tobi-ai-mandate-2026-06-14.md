# Shopify CEO Tobi Lütke: AI Is Now a Baseline Expectation

**Author:** Tobi Lütke, CEO Shopify — internal memo, shared publicly ~April 2026
**Greg Isenberg commentary:** "LEAKED internal memo from CEO of Shopify @tobi around AI"

## Summary
Shopify's CEO published an internal memo declaring that "reflexive AI usage is now a baseline expectation at Shopify." Not a suggestion — a requirement with performance review implications. The memo describes AI as a multiplier enabling 100× output and draws an explicit consequence: before requesting headcount, teams must demonstrate why AI cannot do the work.

## The Memo (Key Extracts)

> "Reflexive AI usage is now a baseline expectation at Shopify. It's a tool of all trades today, and will only grow in importance. Frankly, I don't think it's feasible to opt out of learning the skill of applying AI in your craft; you are welcome to try, but I want to be honest I cannot see this working out today, and definitely not tomorrow."

**Six directives:**
1. Using AI effectively is a fundamental expectation of everyone — including the CEO and executive team
2. AI must be part of the "GSD Prototype phase" — every project prototype should be dominated by AI exploration
3. AI usage questions will be added to performance and peer review questionnaires
4. Learning is self-directed, but share what you learn across the organisation
5. **Before asking for more headcount**, teams must demonstrate why they cannot get the work done using AI
6. "Everyone means everyone" — this applies to the entire company without exception

**The 10× framing:**
> "AI acts as a multiplier… I've seen people approach implausible tasks, ones we wouldn't even have chosen to tackle before, with reflexive and brilliant usage of AI to get 100× the work done."

**Greg Isenberg's summary of the key reframe:**
> "Hire an AI before you hire a human."
