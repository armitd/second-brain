# Thread: CyrilXBT Obsidian Masterclass (15 Parts)

**Author:** CyrilXBT — Jun 10, 2026

## Summary
CyrilXBT's most complete guide: a 15-part masterclass covering the full philosophy and mechanics of building an Obsidian second brain, including Claude integration via Filesystem MCP and five automated intelligence workflows. Written as "the complete guide nobody else wrote."

## Key Points

### Architecture (10-folder structure)
- 00-INBOX (capture buffer), 01-PERMANENT (atomic notes, the heart), 02-LITERATURE (source notes), 03-MAPS (Maps of Content), 04-PROJECTS, 05-DAILY, 06-RESOURCES, 07-OUTPUTS, 08-COMPOUND (Claude-generated outputs), 09-ARCHIVE, 10-SYSTEM (CLAUDE.md + skills)
- **08-COMPOUND is new** — didn't exist in pre-AI Obsidian setups; it's the intelligence layer made visible

### Four core principles
1. **Retrieval-first** — organise for when you need it, not when you file it
2. **Atomic notes** — one idea, not one topic; written in your words, not theirs
3. **Connection principle** — the value of a note is not what it contains, it's what it connects to; every permanent note needs at least two connections
4. **Output principle** — a vault that never produces output is a hobby

### CLAUDE.md as the intelligence contract
The CLAUDE.md defines Active Theses — positions you currently hold that new information might support or contradict. The thesis monitor is the most underused part of every CLAUDE.md. It's what makes the intelligence layer useful rather than just interesting.

### Five automated intelligence workflows
1. **Morning Brief (6AM)** — reads CLAUDE.md + active projects + yesterday's open loops; produces context before you open your laptop
2. **Evening Capture Processor (8PM)** — files daily captures to project notes, creates permanent notes, stores decisions, carries forward open loops
3. **Nightly Connection Finder (11PM)** — reads notes from last 48 hours, finds strong connections not yet explicit; deposits in 08-COMPOUND
4. **Weekly Pattern Detector (Sunday 6PM)** — finds structural patterns appearing across multiple notes from different sources
5. **Monthly Synthesis Generator (1st)** — synthesises any topic with 10+ permanent notes; output must say something no individual note says

### Six manual Claude prompts
- Connection Finder, Question Answerer, Decision Support, Writing Activator, Thinking Partner, Vault Audit — all prompt Claude to reason across the vault

### What month 6 looks like
The vault at month 6 knows more about your intellectual history than you can hold consciously. "Not to store more. To think better."

## Why It Matters
COG is implementing this architecture. The 08-COMPOUND concept (Claude-generated synthesis as a first-class vault destination) is directly analogous to what process-readwise is building. The Active Theses mechanism in CLAUDE.md is the most actionable structural gap vs. current COG setup.
