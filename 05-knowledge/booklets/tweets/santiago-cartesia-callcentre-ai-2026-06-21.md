# Thread: "There's No Way Call Centers Stay in Business After This"

## Summary

Santiago (@svpino) posted a demo of Cartesia's new Sonic-3.5 (TTS) + Ink-2 (STT) voice models, claiming you cannot tell the AI voice agent from a human. Cartesia's CEO confirmed: Sonic-3.5 and Ink-2 are now the #1 streaming models for text-to-speech and speech-to-text respectively — the only provider with #1 models for both speaking and listening simultaneously. Released June 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- Cartesia is now the #1 streaming TTS and STT provider (as of June 15, 2026)
- Sonic-3.5 (speaking) + Ink-2 (listening) — new architectures for real-time voice agents
- The practitioner reaction ("there's no way") is more reliable than a polished enterprise pitch
- This is an API-based speech layer — consumed by voice agent builders, not a CCaaS platform

## Why It Matters

Directly relevant to the CCOTF virtual agent capability (ABB-C02). The TTS/STT layer in any AI voice agent is what makes it credible in production vs. demo. If Cartesia represents the current quality ceiling, any CCOTF evaluation of CCaaS virtual agent capabilities should ask: which vendors support Cartesia as a speech provider, and what does native TTS/STT quality look like by comparison? Already on the Competitive Watchlist.
