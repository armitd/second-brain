# Thread: Andrej Karpathy & the 3D Knowledge Galaxy — Leopardracer / CyrilXBT

## Summary
Leopardracer describes copying Karpathy's approach to build a custom 3D knowledge graph (378 notes, 1,854 nodes, 3,856 edges) that "thinks back" — surfacing hidden connections on demand. CyrilXBT's reply thread expands this into a practical Obsidian + Claude + N8N guide for building a vault with automatic capture, Claude-powered connection surfacing, and daily briefing automation.

## Key Points
- Karpathy's second brain is bespoke — not Notion, Roam, or Obsidian plugins — built from scratch as a graph where any node click surfaces hidden connections across the entire knowledge base.
- "The gap between people who consume knowledge and people who compound it isn't talent. It's architecture."
- CyrilXBT's four-layer stack: Capture (Readwise, Airr, Whisper, Telegram bot) → Pipeline (N8N automation) → Obsidian (permanent storage) → Claude (intelligence/connection layer).
- Five-folder structure: inbox, notes, ideas, projects, CLAUDE.md. Intentionally minimal — complexity in folder structure kills systems.
- Daily brief runs automatically via N8N at 6am: Claude surfaces 3 connections, 1 pattern, 1 question worth sitting with. Weekly synthesis builds a thesis across the week's captures.
