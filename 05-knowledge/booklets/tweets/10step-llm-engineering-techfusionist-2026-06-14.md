# Thread: 10-Step LLM Engineering Roadmap

**Author:** Tech Fusionist — Jun 2026

## The Roadmap

1. **Foundations** — tokenization, embeddings, positional encoding
2. **Transformer mechanics** — attention, multi-head attention, transformer blocks
3. **Inference & decoding** — decoding strategies, speculative decoding, KV cache
4. **Efficient LLM architectures** — FlashAttention, long-context, MQA/GQA/MLA
5. **Beyond standard transformers** — MoE, sparse models, state-space models, diffusion LMs
6. **Data & scaling** — data pipelines, synthetic data, scaling laws
7. **Post-training & alignment** — SFT, DPO, RLHF/GRPO, reward models, safety filters
8. **Deployment & optimization** — quantization, serving stacks, monitoring, telemetry
9. **Application-layer AI systems** — RAG pipelines, tool-using agents, multimodal, vision-language
10. **Safety & interpretability** — red teaming, bias/toxicity analysis, governance controls

## Why It Matters
Useful reference if Belron's AI Damage Assessment PoC moves from foundation model API calls (step 9) to custom model work (steps 6–8). Steps 9 and 10 are where a vendor partner like Faculty AI or Datatonic would operate — useful for evaluating their depth. Step 10 (governance controls) maps to the MCP Governance project's scope.
