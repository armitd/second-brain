### Salesforce Going Headless Is Bigger Than People Realise
**Source:** Readwise/Full Document Contents/Tweets/salesforce going headless is bigger than people realize.md

**Key insight:** Salesforce is shifting to a headless architecture where AI agents make API calls rather than humans logging in. This breaks the per-seat licensing model that has defined enterprise software for 30 years. Companies that adapt to agent-based pricing will win; those that don't will face pricing model disruption. **EA implication:** Belron's enterprise software licensing assumptions (Salesforce, ServiceNow, any per-seat SaaS) need revisiting in an agentic world.
