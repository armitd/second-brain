# That Little Voice in Your Head — Mo Gawdat

## What It's About
Mo Gawdat (former Chief Business Officer at Google X) on the relationship between your thoughts and your sense of self. Follow-up to Solve for Happy — argues that you are not your thoughts, and that this distinction is the foundation of happiness and mental freedom.

## Key Highlights
- "Your thoughts don't define you. If you are not the thoughts in your head, then you no longer need to obey. You can debate and question claims that seem to be invalid. You don't even have to listen. Best of all, with a little bit of practice, you can — and should — frequently tell your brain to shut up completely."

## Why It Matters
Mo Gawdat's framework (happiness = reality ≥ expectations) and his background bringing engineering rigour to human wellbeing is a useful lens for thinking about cognitive habits.
