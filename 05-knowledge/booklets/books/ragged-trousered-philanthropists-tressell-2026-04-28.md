# The Ragged Trousered Philanthropists — Robert Tressell

## What It's About
Classic 1914 novel following a group of house painters in Edwardian England. A foundational text of British socialism — the protagonist tries to explain capitalism and exploitation to his fellow workers, who are too conditioned by the system to listen. Still widely read and influential on the British left.
