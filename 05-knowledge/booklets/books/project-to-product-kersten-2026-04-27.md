# Project to Product — Mik Kersten

## What It's About
Kersten's Flow Framework: argues digital transformation fails when organisations fund projects instead of products. Electrical grids and assembly lines were infrastructure for previous industrial cycles; software and data are infrastructure for this one.

## Key Highlights
- Electrical grids, assembly lines were infrastructure underpinning previous technological ecosystems — digital/AI is the current cycle's infrastructure
- The project-to-product shift: move from funding temporary projects to funding permanent product streams with value streams

## Why It Matters
The infrastructure cycle framing is useful for Belron's AI strategy — arguing that AI isn't a project to deliver but an infrastructure layer to fund continuously. Also connects to the Flow method (Goulding/Shaughnessy) on organisational flow.
