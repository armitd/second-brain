# Fix Your Fatigue — Karina Antram

## What It's About
A functional medicine approach to chronic fatigue. Central mechanism: mitochondrial function and ATP production. Addresses why people feel persistently tired despite adequate sleep.

## Key Highlights
- Mitochondria oxidize carbohydrates, fats, and amino acids to produce ATP
- Cellular respiration: glucose + oxygen → carbon dioxide + water + energy (ATP)
- Fatigue as a mitochondrial efficiency problem, not just a sleep problem
