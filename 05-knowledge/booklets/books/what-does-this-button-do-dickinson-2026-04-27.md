# What Does This Button Do? — Bruce Dickinson

## What It's About
Autobiography of Bruce Dickinson — Iron Maiden frontman, commercial airline pilot, entrepreneur, and fencer. Covers his childhood, the rise of Maiden, his solo career, his brief departure from the band, and his return. Unusually candid and wide-ranging for a rock autobiography.

## Key Highlights
- On piloting a vintage Fokker aircraft: "It was like taxiing a combination of a venetian blind and a bookcase. Simply starting the Fokker was hazardous." — characteristically dry Dickinson humour, and a window into his aviation life alongside music

## Why It Matters
Personal interest — music autobiography. Dickinson is a more interesting person than most rock bios reveal; this one does justice to the full picture.
