# Building a Second Brain — Tiago Forte

## What It's About
The foundational text for the Second Brain / PARA method — systematic capture, organise, distil, express (CODE) applied to personal knowledge management using digital tools.

## Key Highlights
- **PARA system:** Projects (short-term with a deadline), Areas (ongoing responsibilities), Resources (future reference), Archives (inactive). COG's vault structure mirrors this.
- Every piece of information maps to one of four categories — the constraint is the point. Deciding once, not every time.

## Why It Matters
This book is the conceptual ancestor of COG. Tiago Forte's CODE methodology (Capture, Organise, Distil, Express) maps directly to how COG processes Readwise content into second brain knowledge. PARA is why COG's folders are numbered 00-06 in the order they are.
