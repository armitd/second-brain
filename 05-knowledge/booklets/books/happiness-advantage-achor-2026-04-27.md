# The Happiness Advantage — Shawn Achor

## What It's About
Argues that happiness is not the result of success — it's the precondition for it. Drawing on positive psychology research at Harvard, Achor presents seven principles for rewiring the brain toward positivity and using that advantage to drive performance at work and in life.
