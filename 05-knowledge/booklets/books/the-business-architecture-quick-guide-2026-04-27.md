# The Business Architecture Quick Guide — Business Architecture Guild

## What It's About
A practitioner's introduction to business architecture — capabilities, value streams, and how business architecture relates to (but differs from) enterprise architecture.

## Key Highlights
- Clausewitz: "The first task of any theory is to clarify terms and concepts that are confused… Only after agreement has been reached regarding terms and concepts can we hope to consider the issues easily and clearly."
- Business architecture as the bridge between strategy and execution — capability maps, value streams, information maps

## Why It Matters
The Clausewitz framing applies to AI governance work: before governing AI agents, define what "agent" means. The capability map discipline is useful for scoping which Belron capabilities are candidates for AI augmentation.
