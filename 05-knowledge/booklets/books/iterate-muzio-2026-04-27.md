# Iterate — Ed Muzio

## What It's About
A management system for iterative output delivery. The VSO (Verbalized Stated Output) concept: teams define and publicly commit to their outputs rather than their inputs or activities.

## Key Highlights
- **VSO (Verbalized Stated Output):** 3-7 measurable output statements that account for ~80% of a group's work. Takes 60-90 seconds to say aloud. You share it constantly — in meetings, with clients, with colleagues.
- The VSO is an output commitment, not an activity list — the distinction is the point
- Lead with your VSO: "When you introduce yourself in a meeting, you lead with your VSO."

## Why It Matters
VSO discipline is useful for AI project framing at Belron — defining outcomes, not activities ("we will process N claims per day" not "we will implement an AI pipeline"). Also relevant to how AI CoPs articulate their value.
