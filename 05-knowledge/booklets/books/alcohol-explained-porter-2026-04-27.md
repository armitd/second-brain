# Alcohol Explained — William Porter

## What It's About
A clinical and psychological explanation of how alcohol works, why it's addictive, and why attempts to moderate fail. Written from the perspective of someone who has stopped.

## Key Highlights
- "When we are constantly taking a drink to take the edge off our feelings whenever we experience anything negative, we are anaesthetising feelings rather than facing them — with the result that we never learn to deal with them."
