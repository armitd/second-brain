# Digital Minimalism — Cal Newport

## What It's About
A philosophy of intentional technology use — only adopting tools that serve your deeply held values, and being willing to miss out on the lesser benefits of other tools. Companion to Deep Work.

## Key Highlights
- Pascal: "All of humanity's problems stem from man's inability to sit quietly in a room alone."
- The attention economy exploits the same psychological vulnerabilities as slot machines
- Solitude deficit: we've lost the ability to be alone with our thoughts

## Why It Matters
Ironic context for a COG session, but relevant to how AI tools get adopted. The risk of AI-as-distraction (another tool that feels productive but isn't) versus AI-as-leverage (tools that serve specific deep-work goals). COG is designed on minimalist principles — do less, better.
