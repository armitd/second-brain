# Working Backwards — Colin Bryar and Bill Carr

## What It's About
Inside account of Amazon's operating principles: Working Backwards (start from the customer, write the press release first), the six-page narrative memo format, single-threaded ownership, and how Amazon's culture drives product clarity.

## Key Highlights
- **Document length restriction as forcing function:** Restricting memo length develops better thinkers and communicators. The constraint is the point — not the length itself.
- The narrative memo format (not decks) forces rigour: you have to actually think, not just arrange bullets.

## Why It Matters
The "document length as forcing function" insight is directly applicable to Belron's AI strategy communications — one-pagers and six-pagers force clearer thinking than slide decks. Also relevant to the Shopify one-pager opportunity: the constraint of one page forces the argument to be coherent.
