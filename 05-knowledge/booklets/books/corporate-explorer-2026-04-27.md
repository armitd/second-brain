# Corporate Explorer — Binns, O'Reilly, Tushman

## What It's About
How large organisations can build innovation capability from within. The "Corporate Explorer" is the archetype of an internal entrepreneur — someone who can navigate the mothership while building something new.

## Key Highlights
- **Strategic ambition:** Good strategic ambition is emotionally compelling (engages passion), logically connected (to overall business strategy, so exploration feels integral not alien), and linked to performance (scale of aspiration appropriate to the disruption threat)
- Strategic ambition inspires more Corporate Explorers and enables their success through five mechanisms
- Innovation built outside the core: the ambidextrous organisation model

## Why It Matters
Directly relevant to Belron's AI capability building — Darren's role sits at the Corporate Explorer intersection: navigating the mothership while building AI-native ways of working. The "emotionally compelling + logically connected" framing for strategic ambition is useful for positioning the AI strategy.
