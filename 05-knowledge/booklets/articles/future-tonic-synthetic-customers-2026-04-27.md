# Future Tonic: AI-Driven Synthetic Customers

## Summary
Future Tonic builds interactive AI-driven "Synthetic Customers" — dynamic personas that simulate realistic customer responses to test ideas, surface conflicts, and build team empathy without always requiring live customer interviews. Combines human-centred research with GenAI to keep insights realistic and actionable.

## Key Highlights
- Synthetic Customers = AI personas trained to simulate realistic customer responses at scale
- Use cases: idea testing, conflict surfacing, empathy building for product/service teams
- Combines qualitative research methodology with generative AI — not a replacement for real customers but a complement
- Reduces the time and cost of early-stage customer validation

## Why It Matters
Directly relevant to the Contact Centre of the Future project. A CCOTF development process that includes synthetic customer testing would allow rapid iteration on AI-assisted contact centre interfaces before real customer exposure. This is a concrete, buildable capability — not just a concept.
