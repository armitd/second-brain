# 2025: The State of Consumer AI — Menlo Ventures

## Summary
Menlo Ventures annual consumer AI report. Over half of American adults have used AI recently; 91% of AI users default to their favourite general AI tool for nearly every task before trying specialist alternatives. ChatGPT (28%) and Gemini (23%) dominate. Adoption is driven by embedded products with existing distribution, not standalone AI apps.

## Key Highlights
- 91% of AI users reach for a general AI tool first regardless of task
- ChatGPT 28% share, Gemini 23% — both driven by existing platform distribution (OpenAI integrations, Google Search)
- Consumers adopt AI when it clearly helps them do things better, faster, easier — utility over novelty
- AI is still underused for common personal activities — significant room for growth
