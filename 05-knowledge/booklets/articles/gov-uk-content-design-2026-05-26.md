# Content Design: Planning, Writing and Managing Content

## Summary
GOV.UK's house guide for writing online — the public-sector reference for plain-English, user-led content. Core principles: write for scanning (not reading), use plain words and short sentences so specialists and non-specialists understand alike, and make titles/summaries self-contained so users can judge at a glance whether the page is what they want.
