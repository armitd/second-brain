# What Is the Role of Media in a Healthy Democracy? How Is This Role Impacted by Social Media and Fake News?

## Summary
Argues that media's core democratic function is informing the public and holding power accountable, but mainstream media often serves elite interests while social media gives marginalized voices a platform. Fake news and corporate control of online discourse are framed as threats to that democratic function.

## Key Highlights
- "Always look for the truth from the ground up, rarely from the top down. Journalists are never really journalists if they are agents of power... Real Journalists are agents of people." — quoting John Pilger
