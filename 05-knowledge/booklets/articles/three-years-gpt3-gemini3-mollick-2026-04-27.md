# Three Years From GPT-3 to Gemini 3 — Ethan Mollick

## Summary
Ethan Mollick traces the arc from simple chatbots (GPT-3 era) to powerful agentic systems (Gemini 3 era) in just three years. Modern AI can write code, run tools, and conduct complex research at a graduate-student level — a fundamental shift from AI that fixes mistakes to AI that acts as a collaborative digital coworker still requiring human guidance.

## Key Highlights
- The shift from chatbot → agent happened faster than most enterprise AI strategies anticipated
- Gemini 3 represents the "collaborative digital coworker" paradigm — not fully autonomous, but substantively capable
- Human guidance remains essential: the model operates best with a human directing intent and reviewing outputs
- The three-year timeline is a useful calibration: what was impossible in 2022 is now standard

## Why It Matters
A useful framing document for Belron stakeholder conversations about AI adoption pace. Mollick is widely cited and credible — his "digital coworker" framing is more accurate and less threatening than "AI replacing jobs," making it useful for change management contexts.
